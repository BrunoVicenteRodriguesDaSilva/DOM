Straw Hat Pirates

The crew is formed by ten members, all found while their captain, Monkey D. Luffy, was in his journey to become the Pirates's King!

The crew:

Luffy Zoro Usopp
Sanji Nami Chopper
Robin Franky Brook
Jinbe

Luffy

The Straw Hat's captain, who has eaten the Gomu Gomu No Mi, a Devil Fruit that transforms the user into rubber. He looks after to become the Pirates' King, founding the treasure left by Gol D. Roger.

Zoro

The first to enter to the crew, after he was saved by Luffy. Zoro fights with 3 swords.

Usopp

The second Luffy's crewmate, who uses a slingshot to fire at his enemies.

Sanji

The Straw Hat's chef, responsible for making the delicious foods that the crew eat. Fights with his legs, because he doesn't want to hurt his hands.

Nami

The fifth member to oficially enter the crew. She's responsible for the navigation and uses the weather to fight.

Chopper

The doctor of the tripulation. It's a deer, who ate the Hito Hito No Mi, and now can change his form.

Robin

The Straw Hat's archeologist, ate the Hana Hana No Mi, and can make parts of her body appear at anywhere she wants/sees. Robin also can read Poneglyphs.

Franky

Franky it's a human, who has modified his body to become a weapon, and now is a robot. He's the shiwright of the crew.

Brook

With the most important in the ship, there is Brook, who is the musician. He ate the Yomi Yomi No Mi, and now he's a skeleton who fights with a sword.

Jinbe

The last member to enter the crew, until now. He's a fish-man and the Helmsman of the crew. Jinbe is alsoa a specialist in the fish-man Karate.
